What We Offer

Services Built
for Country

CASA-accredited drone operations, AI environmental analytics, and cultural consultancy — delivered by Indigenous custodians who know this land.

🌿

Mangrove & Wetland Monitoring

AI-powered aerial classification of mangrove species and health across Traditional Owner sea country. Multispectral imaging with machine learning delivers 97% verified accuracy — deployable across any TO Country in Australia and internationally.

RMIT Partnership 97% Accuracy Sea Country
🔥

Cultural Burn Planning

Drone-assisted fire-risk mapping and post-burn assessment guided by the 6 Seasons Methodology. Thermal imaging combined with Traditional Ecological Knowledge identifies optimal burn windows, reducing risk and restoring Country with cultural integrity.

6 Seasons Methodology Thermal Imaging
🌧️

Invasive Species & Weed Control

Aerial detection of pond apple, rubber vine, and other invasives across Traditional Owner Country. Precision targeting via drone-guided injection programs dramatically reduces chemical load while protecting native vegetation.

Pond Apple Far North QLD
🤖

AI & Environmental Data Analytics

Automated species identification, predictive ecosystem modelling, and interactive environmental dashboards. We turn aerial imagery into decision-ready intelligence for land managers, government agencies, and research partners.

Machine Learning Dashboards
📡

Under-Canopy Autonomous Systems

Next-generation autonomous drone navigation beneath rainforest canopy — monitoring root systems, creek lines, and biodiversity indicators invisible from above. Currently in R&D with RMIT University, Semester 2 2026.

RMIT R&D Autonomous Nav
🎓

Training & Workforce Development

CASA RePL drone certification pathways, AI environmental workshops, and youth mentoring programs. Building the next generation of Indigenous environmental technology leaders across Australia.

CASA RePL Youth Programs
