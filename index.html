What We Do

Four Pillars of
Country Care

From mangrove canopies to rock art sites — we combine drone technology, AI, and Traditional Knowledge to protect Country.

🌿

Environmental Resilience

Mangrove monitoring, biosecurity surveillance, marine debris mapping, cultural burn planning, and invasive species control across Traditional Owner Country.

🪶

Cultural Heritage

Rock art documentation, heritage drone mapping, sacred site preservation, and Traditional Owner engagement — keeping culture strong.

🤖

Technology & Innovation

AI mangrove classification (97% accuracy), government technology trials, Indigenous data sovereignty, and drone certification training.

🎓

Education & Empowerment

Junior Ranger programs, school drone training, cultural mentoring, men's wellbeing programs, and digital skills pathways for the next generation.

Our Mission

Protecting Country Through
Traditional Knowledge & AI

"Artificial intelligence doesn't replace Traditional Knowledge — it amplifies it."

Our AI systems are trained on Country, guided by Traditional Owners, and built to translate thousands of years of ecological knowledge into data the modern world can act on — while ensuring that data always serves Country, community, and culture first.

Protecting Country. Empowering Communities. Creating the Future.
