Executive Summary

This pilot demonstrates an end-to-end capability: DJI Matrice 4T RTK aerial capture of mangrove canopy across three target species (Yellow, Orange and Red mangroves), photogrammetric processing into orthomosaics and textured 3D models, and classification against RMIT University's peer-reviewed species-identification model — with every flight archived to a chain-of-custody data store and made available through an access-code-gated client portal.

All three species — Red, Yellow and Orange Mangroves, all captured at Coquette Point Road on 13 July 2026 — are now complete end-to-end: capture, 3D reconstruction, and secure upload. Every flight's orthomosaic, textured 3D model and processing quality report is live in the client portal today.
